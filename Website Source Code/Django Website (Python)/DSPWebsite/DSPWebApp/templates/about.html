How to play:

When you first join the game you will be greated with two buttons for each of the puzzles and also a keyboard and screen.

The buttons in the main menu will take you to either puzzle, depending on which you click. Both are labeled for convenience.

The keyboard allows a player to enter there name, this information will then be used to display score data on the puzzles respective Leaderboard page.

8 Queens:

8 Queens is played by placing all 8 queens on the chess board, in such a way that none of them can attack each other.

When all 8 queens have been played press the button on the right of where you spawn to check to see if your solution is correct.

2048:

Move and merge cubes to produce new cubes. When two cubes of the same value collide they create a new cube with a value of the two cubes used to create it added together.

You win by: Move and match cubes until you get a 2048 cube. You gain 10 points for each merge you make. You lose by: Filling up the grid and having no moves or merges left to make.
